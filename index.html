Team GB Bouldering

The home of competition climbing in the UK. The GB Climbing Team is run by the British Mountaineering Council (BMC). The BMC is the national governing body for sport climbing in the UK and is recognised by the International Federation for Sport Climbing (IFSC). The BMC manages the GB Climbing Teams, Talent and Performance pathways, national and international competition events in the UK, and proudly promotes the future of our sport.
